ReHacted: Spy School is a 24 hour Hackathon for the DMV. So close to our capital, this event is inspired by spy media, everything from Men In Black, Spy Kids, James Bond. Our own globally estembed intelligence government agencies such as the CIA, FBI and the NSA! Your mission, if you choose to accept it. Is to build the best spy software or gadget, competing with all the other teams to win the coveted Valedictorian and Salutatorian positions at our school! To see who is ready to move on, onto the field as an agent. This once and a lifetime coding competition will be 100% free to attend, helping make it accessible to the less fortunate. For the entire 24 hours, we will have workshops, guest speakers, and experienced chaperones willing to help, making the event even more amazing and beginner friendly! So come on down to our secret bunker, get your Custom Id badges and get hacking! 24 hours filled with free fun, food and fantastic learning experiences.
